Brand Foundation

Paramount Bay Homebuyers Branding and Design Language

A practical, page-by-page design reference that captures our Florida Gulf Coast tone: light, friendly, sunny, trustworthy, and easy to navigate.

Use this system whenever we build headers, landing pages, forms, or section templates so every page feels like one clear, local brand.

Light Friendly Sunny Approachable Trustworthy Simple Navigation

"Friendly local cash buyer who makes selling simple."

Target emotional impression

Brand Visual Direction

The visual story should feel like Tampa Bay: warm sunlight, palm trees, coastal homes, and relaxed neighborhoods. Avoid dark, corporate, or aggressive styling.

Do

  • Keep layouts airy with generous breathing space.
  • Use rounded elements and subtle shadows.
  • Prioritize easy scanning and friendly copy blocks.
  • Lead with bright neighborhood photography.

Avoid

  • Dense sections, cluttered modules, and heavy borders.
  • Dark mood photography and gloomy homes.
  • Aggressive color combinations and harsh contrasts.
  • Heavy gradients and over-animated blocks.

Typography System

Headings should be bold and rounded in Poppins or Montserrat. Body copy should stay airy and highly readable in Open Sans, Inter, or Nunito with line-height between 1.5 and 1.7.

Heading Font

H1: 42-48px

H2: 32px

H3: 24px

Use bold, friendly headline rhythm with short lines for easy scanning.

Body Font

Body text at 18px should feel open, clear, and conversational for homeowners in high-stress selling situations.

Small text: 15px for secondary details and helper copy.

Never use condensed fonts or dense paragraph blocks.

Layout and Section Rhythm

Every page should feel spacious with minimal borders, rounded cards, and soft shadows. Alternate section backgrounds to keep long pages bright and easy to follow.

Section 1 White #FFFFFF
Section 2 Soft Blue #F4FAFF
Section 3 Soft Sand #F7E6B0

Header and Hero Blueprint

Keep navigation sticky with a white or very light blue background (#F7FBFF). Use a left-aligned logo, the primary nav links, and a right-side "Get Cash Offer" CTA in Sunset Orange.

Mobile Priority

Mobile-first behavior should include sticky call actions, large tap targets, simplified nav, and vertical section flow.

Animation Guidelines

Motion should be minimal and supportive only. Keep interactions calm and intentional.

Allowed

  • Button hover glow
  • Subtle icon bounce
  • Light fade-in section reveals

Avoid

  • Heavy motion and long animated sequences
  • Sliding panels across most page sections
  • Distracting transitions that slow scanning

Implementation Checklist

Before launch, confirm typography sizes, CTA color rules, sticky mobile call action, and section rhythm (white, soft blue, soft sand) are all applied.
