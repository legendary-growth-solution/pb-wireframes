Paramount Bay Homebuyers Web Design Guidelines


Website Design Technical Specification
Brand Visual Direction
The website should reflect the Florida Gulf Coast lifestyle: warm sun, palm trees, coastal homes, and relaxed neighborhoods.
Design feeling should be:
• light
• friendly
• sunny
• approachable
• trustworthy
• simple to navigate
Avoid anything that feels corporate, dark, or aggressive.
Target emotional impression:
"Friendly local cash buyer who makes selling simple."

Image Style

 • dark photography
